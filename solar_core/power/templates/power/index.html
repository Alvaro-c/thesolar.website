Power generation results using a 30W solar panel facing North in the city of Berlin

Under development

This project consist in a web server entirely powered by a solar panel.

Battery level

The solar panel charges a Lead Acid 12V battery with 7000mAh capacity. That battery then powers a Raspberry pi Zero.

The battery is considered charged when it is over 13.89V and discharged when the voltage drops to 11.6V

The Voltage is measured with a Voltage sensor connected to an Arduino Nano

Measurements

The following measurements are result of a INA219 sensor connected to the same Arduino Nano as the Voltage sensor.

The Raspberry Zero has a consumption of 5V and ~230mA, therefore 1.15W per hour. The system is self-sufficien when the power generated during the day is over ~28W.
